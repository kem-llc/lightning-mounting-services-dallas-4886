About Lightning Mounting Services

To provide Dallas with reliable, high-quality handyman services that make life easier.

Lightning Mounting Services was founded in Dallas with a simple mission: deliver fast, professional handyman work at fair prices. What started as a TV mounting side gig quickly grew into a full-service handyman business serving homeowners and businesses across the city.

Today, we're proud to maintain a perfect 5.0 rating by focusing on customer satisfaction, punctuality, and craftsmanship. Every job, big or small, gets the same attention to detail. We're not happy until you're thrilled.
